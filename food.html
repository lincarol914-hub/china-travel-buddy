Quick Tips

Eating in China

Essential things to know before you sit down to eat.

📱

Scan to Order

Many restaurants use QR codes to order food. Have WeChat ready to scan.

🌶️

Spice Levels Vary

Sichuan and Hunan cuisine can be very spicy. Ask "不要辣" (not spicy) if needed.

💰

Cash is Rare

Most restaurants accept Alipay or WeChat Pay only. Have your app set up before dining.

🥢

Share Everything

Chinese dining is communal — dishes are shared across the table. Order a variety!
