Daily Chinese Phrases

Simple, practical phrases to help you communicate confidently while travelling in China.

💡

Pro Tip: Show, Don't Say

If pronunciation feels tricky, simply show your phone screen with the Chinese characters to locals — they'll understand immediately. Screenshots of these phrases are a lifesaver!

Eating Out

English
Chinese
Pinyin
Hello / Hi
你好
Nǐ hǎo
I would like to order this.
我想点这个。
Wǒ xiǎng diǎn zhège.
Do you have an English menu?
有英文菜单吗？
Yǒu yīngwén càidān ma?
Not spicy, please.
不要辣。
Bù yào là.
I can't eat peanuts.
我不能吃花生。
Wǒ bù néng chī huāshēng.
The bill, please.
买单，谢谢。
Mǎi dān, xièxie.
